Video Project

For this video project, I wanted to show the evolution of music and exaplain just how popular that particular song was in a certain decade. To show this, I included 2 of some the most popular songs of that particular decade based off how many plays and views they got on streaming services. I also based off a songs popularitiy if it still gets played to this day or has a clear influence on music today. This video was a way for me to practice my video editing and certain film shots as well.
