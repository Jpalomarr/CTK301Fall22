About Me Introduction

Hello, my name is Josh Palomar and I like creating audio and video projects involving adding audio to specific video projects. The types of music that I'm mostly influenced by is all types of genres, but mainly stuff such as indie rock, hip-hop, and pop. Some of my favorite bands that have influenced me are bands such as Radiohead, Arcitc Monkeys, Deftones, and Tame Impala. I mostly play the guitar, but sometimes I create synth tracks mainly because some of my favorite bands have this type of sound in their music.
